Welcome to Bethany Surf Shop!

Since 1980 we have been the premier destination for all your surfing, bodyboarding, skim-boarding, and stand-up paddle board needs while visiting Bethany Beach, Rehoboth Beach, Dewey Beach, Fenwick Island, and Ocean City. Our shop has everything you need to experience the thrill and adventure of the Delaware Seashore. Our main store on 99 Garfield Parkway is open Thursday - Monday at 10 am. We are closed Tuesday and Wednesdays through April. Our outlet location will reopen May of 2024.
